Donate to the ACB

In the spirit of brotherhood and continued support of our undergraduate brothers we invite you to donate to the Delta Sigma Phi Alpha Iota Alumni Corporation Board (ACB). Our intent is to use these funds in support of the undergraduate chapter for three purposes:

  • Leadership Development
  • Housing
  • Scholarship

We welcome one-time and recurring donations of all sizes. We thank you for your commitment to giving back to Alpha Iota as we strive to instill the values of culture, harmony, and friendship. YITBOS!
